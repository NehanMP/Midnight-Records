Born Pink

album

Discover the artistry of Blackpink, the world's most prominent girl group, through their captivating second studio album, "Born Pink," which graced the airwaves on September 16, 2022, under the joint collaboration of our Entertainment and Interscope Records. Following the success of their previous album, "The Album," in 2020, "Born Pink" signifies their triumphant return with a full-length musical offering.


This mesmerizing album spans a total of twenty-four minutes and comprises a collection of eight exceptional tracks, with three of them bearing explicit labels. "Born Pink" effortlessly weaves together an eclectic range of genres, including hip-hop, pop, disco, ballad, alt-pop, pop-rock, stadium rock, punk, EDM, bubblegum pop, and even tasteful classical interludes. The result is a groundbreaking fusion that showcases Blackpink's versatility and unrivaled talent.


The album's dynamic progression is captivating, with the first half emanating an aura of "extreme confidence" and an "untouchable exterior," a testament to the group's unwavering charisma. The second half delves into introspective and "emotionally charged" ballads, offering a glimpse into their vulnerabilities and emotional depth. Throughout the album, the group's mastery of the English language shines brightly, further amplifying the impact of their powerful lyrics and delivering a "faster" and "shinier" experience compared to their previous work.


Checkmate

album

Under the banner of MR Entertainment, Itzy presented "Checkmate," their fifth EP that has garnered significant attention. Released on July 15, 2022, in collaboration with JYP Entertainment and Republic Records, the EP comprises seven impressive tracks, including the lead single "Sneakers".


The success of "Checkmate" has been extraordinary, claiming the top position on South Korea's Circle Album Chart with an astounding 700,000 copies sold in the first week of its release. This achievement marks Itzy's fourth number-one album on the chart and their best-selling album to date. Moreover, the EP received the esteemed "Million" certification from the KMCA for surpassing 1,000,000 copies sold in September. Internationally, "Checkmate" made a significant impact, debuting at number eight on the Billboard 200, marking Itzy's first top-ten album in the US.


Psycho

album

Red Velvet presented "Psycho," an enchanting song that serves as the lead single from their first compilation album, The ReVe Festival: Finale 2022. This album stands as the triumphant conclusion to their illustrious album trilogy, The ReVe Festival. Crafted by renowned composers Andrew Scott, Cazzi Opeia, and EJAE, "Psycho" artfully blends R and B, pop, trap, and future bass elements, delving into the intricacies of a complex romantic relationship. Accompanied by a captivating gothic-themed music video, the single was digitally released on December 23, 2022.


Critics showered "Psycho" with praise, commending its "smooth and sultry" delivery, as well as its "haunting" and "catchy yet structured" production. Despite its late release in 2022, the song was hailed as one of the finest K-pop tracks of the year. Its success was further solidified by topping charts in Singapore and North America, becoming Red Velvet's second number-one song on the Billboard World Digital Songs chart and elevating them to the ranks of girl groups with the most top-10 entries on the ranking, alongside 2NE1. Additionally, the song achieved remarkable positions on various international charts, including Malaysia and New Zealand. Notably, it spent three consecutive weeks in the top positions on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart, marking Red Velvet's 12th domestic top-10 entry.


Red Velvet's "Psycho" exudes a mesmerizing allure, showcasing the group's undeniable talent and artistry, making it a standout addition to their distinguished discography under our label.
